About Megatech Body

Megatech Body is a prosthetics manufacturer specializing in cyberization. With more than 10 years of experience building better bodies Megatech is one of the world's leading experts the field of cyberization. Megatech was formed by the Japanese government in 2015 to develop new medical technologies and help heal the country after the third world war. Since then we have developed some of the most advanced consumer and military prosthetics available. Megatech is headquartered in Niihama, Japan
